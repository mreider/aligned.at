Our Approach

A Three-Step Process

In-Depth Interviews

I conduct structured interviews, each lasting at least an hour. I start with the most product-focused founder and then sequentially engage with sales, support, product, and engineering teams. Finally, we interview a successful and satisfied customer. This process aligns the company’s vision with market positioning, customer feedback, and actual product development, providing a comprehensive understanding of how the product is conceived, communicated, and executed across the organization.

Targeted Follow-Ups

After pinpointing gaps and inconsistencies from initial interviews, I hold targeted follow-up sessions. These sessions delve deeper into areas of concern, maintaining a positive and constructive tone to keep discussions open and non-confrontational. I approach these conversations with journalistic curiosity, exploring the disconnect between an organization's product goals and market needs.

Comprehensive Report

I synthesize insights from initial interviews and follow-ups into a comprehensive report. The report evaluates the organization's health in terms of product strategy, alignment, and execution. It highlights strengths, identifies improvement areas, and provides targeted suggestions for enhancement. You can view an example report based on a previous project here.
