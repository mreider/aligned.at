Our Approach

A Three-Step Process

In-Depth Interviews

We conduct ten structured interviews, each lasting an hour, beginning with the most product-focused founder, to capture the initial vision. We then move to sales, support, product, and engineering teams sequentially. This process ensures alignment between the company’s vision, market position, customer feedback, and actual product development. The goal is to comprehensively understand how the product is conceived, communicated, and executed across the organization.

Targeted Follow-Ups

After identifying inconsistencies and gaps from the initial interviews, we conduct targeted follow-up sessions. These follow-ups are designed to probe deeper into areas of concern in a positive and constructive manner, ensuring that discussions remain open and non-confrontational. We approach these conversations with the curiosity of a journalist, seeking clarity and further insights without placing anyone on the defensive. This step is crucial for refining our understanding and helping your team align more closely with the company's goals and market needs.

Comprehensive Report

We synthesize all insights gathered from the initial interviews and follow-ups into a detailed report. This document assesses the organization's health from the perspectives of product strategy, alignment, and execution. It highlights strengths, pinpoints areas needing improvement, and offers targeted suggestions for enhancement. The report includes both overarching insights that affect the entire organization and specific recommendations tailored to individual departments, fostering a holistic approach to refinement and growth.
